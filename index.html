Welcome to Shree Suryodaya Public Secondary School

Established in 2054 B.S., Shree Suryodaya Public Secondary School is committed to providing quality education in the far western region of Nepal. Located in Bansaha, Kanchanpur, the school offers a nurturing environment for students to grow academically and socially.

With modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on extracurricular activities, the school has consistently ranked among the top institutions in the district. We take pride in our legacy and welcome new students and parents to become part of the Suryodaya family.

Academic Excellence

At Shree Suryodaya, we follow a comprehensive curriculum designed to foster critical thinking and academic growth. Our dedicated teachers employ innovative teaching methodologies to make learning engaging and effective.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Well-structured syllabus aligned with national standards.
  • Focus on practical learning and application of knowledge.
  • Regular assessments to track student progress.

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in the holistic development of our students. A wide range of extracurricular activities are offered to nurture their talents and interests.

  • Sports: Cricket, Volleyball, Badminton, Athletics
  • Clubs: Eco- Club, Arts Club, Sports Club, JRC Club,It Club
  • Cultural Events: Annual Day, Festivals, Competitions, sports week

Our Facilities

We provide a conducive learning environment with modern infrastructure and essential facilities.

  • Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms
  • Well-equipped computer laboratories
  • A library with a wide collection of books
  • Large playground for sports and recreational activities
  • Own well facilited school buses

About Us

Shree Suryodaya Public Secondary School is committed to providing quality education in a safe and nurturing environment. Our goal is to foster academic excellence, personal growth, and a strong sense of community. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for the challenges of the modern world, while also emphasizing values such as respect, discipline, and teamwork.

Our school has been established with the vision to impart quality education with values. We have achieved remarkable milestones, with thousands of alumni making their mark in various sectors. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure and a team of dedicated teachers make us a leading educational institution in the region.

We continuously strive to innovate our teaching methods and integrate technology to provide a dynamic learning experience. Our strong community ties and parental involvement are key to our students' success.
